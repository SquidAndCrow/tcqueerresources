tcqueer.com is a vetted and curated list of queer resources in Tri-Cities, Washington. Some resources may be based outside of the Tri-Cities, but all resources will be available to local folks. There are other folks who have done a ton of work to curate resource listings of their own, and while we're extremely thankful to link over to them, we do not maintain those listings and can not update them.

Know of a safe resource that isn't on the list? Submit it through our submission form.

Think a resource should be removed for safety reasons? Let us know through our removal form.

Be safe folks, we love you.
